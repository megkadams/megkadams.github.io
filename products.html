Products

All of the Lightcloud products are built and tested with the same industrial strength of all RAB products.

Gateway

The Lightcloud Gateway is at the center of your system. It provides a reliable cellular connection to all of your other devices as well as allows you to control your lights from anywhere. It also has a backup power-supply built in to the enclosure, so if the power goes out your system doesn’t.

Controller

A Lightcloud Controller has both the switching and dimming capabilities and will be the main unit for your system. Weatherproof, fireproof and designed for easy install and discoverability, you’ll want to hook up everything to it.

Occupancy Sensor

On when you’re here and off when you’re not, the Lightcloud Occupancy Sensor is a key device when you want to save energy. Slim, smart and capable of switching circuits, it’s a must–have addition to any lighting setup.

Daylight Harvester

With the Lightcloud Daylight Harvester, there’s no more worrying about the light levels in windowed rooms. Designed to be inconspicuous, just set it and forget it, except when you look back and realize that it’s automatically saving you lots of energy.
